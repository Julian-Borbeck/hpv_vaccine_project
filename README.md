# HPV Vaccine Project

Multi-faceted analysis of HPV vaccine
- How vaccine affordability influence its distribution?
- How vaccine distribution affects cancer rates in the world?
- Are there some biological repercussions on the virus itself (at a micro biological point of view)?

## Project Structure

### Socio-Econ
Analysis of HPV vaccine access, prices, and uptake across countries and their relationship to socio-economic characteristics.

### Bio_surface
Analysis of HPV vaccine coverage and HPV related cancer incidence
Visualization of vaccine trends over the years for each WHO region

### Bio_deep
Biological deep analysis component.
